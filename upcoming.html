Upcoming Institutions

The Kerala Group plans to start Nursing, Paramedical, and Management Colleges in its Trivandrum campus. This initiative aims to provide students with a comprehensive education and training in healthcare and management.

Kerala Academy of Nursing

Located in Kandala Near Kattakkada, Trivandrum – Kerala.

Kerala Academy of Paramedical Sciences

Focused on advanced paramedical education and training.

Kerala Academy Institute of Management Studies

Offering specialized management programs for future leaders.

Potential Programs


Nursing Programs

BSc Nursing

The BSc Nursing program is a four-year undergraduate course designed to provide students with in-depth knowledge and hands-on training in patient care, medical sciences, and healthcare management. Graduates are prepared for diverse roles in hospitals, clinics, and community healthcare settings.

GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery)

The GNM program is a three-year diploma course focusing on essential nursing skills, patient care, and midwifery. It equips students with practical knowledge and clinical experience to work in hospitals, maternity care, and primary healthcare settings.

Paramedical Programs

BSc MLT (Medical Laboratory Technology)

BSc MLT is a three-year undergraduate program that trains students in diagnostic laboratory techniques. It covers clinical pathology, microbiology, hematology, and biochemistry, enabling graduates to work in hospitals, research labs, and diagnostic centers.

BSc Radiography

BSc Radiography is a three-year program focused on medical imaging techniques such as X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs. Students learn to operate imaging equipment and assist in disease diagnosis, preparing them for roles in hospitals, diagnostic labs, and radiology departments.

BSc Radiology

BSc Radiology is a four-year degree that provides in-depth knowledge of medical imaging and radiation therapy. It includes training in ultrasound, fluoroscopy, and nuclear medicine, preparing graduates for careers in hospitals, cancer treatment centers, and medical imaging facilities.

Management Programs

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

MBA is a two-year postgraduate program that focuses on business management, leadership, and strategic decision-making. It covers areas like finance, marketing, human resources, and operations, preparing graduates for leadership roles in corporate, entrepreneurship, and global business sectors.

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

BBA is a three-year undergraduate course that provides fundamental business knowledge in management, accounting, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It equips students with skills for entry-level managerial positions or higher studies like an MBA.

These programs are designed to meet industry demands, focusing on Practical Training, Research, and Community Engagement.
